\obcproc{Antenna Deployment Process}

This process is responsible for reliable deployment of RF antennas using any available of antenna controller.
The process performed as during the \procref{LEOP} period of the mission. It is started automatically in 40th 
minute of the mission time assuming that this procedure is not disabled in the satellite \procref{Persistent State}.

The configuration of this process is as follows:

\begin{longtable}{l|l|m{6cm}|M{1cm}|M{1.5cm}}
    \toprule
    \textbf{Group} & \textbf{Variable} & \textbf{Description} & \textbf{Size [bits]} & \textbf{Default Value} \\
    \midrule
    \endhead
    AntennaConfiguration & isDeploymentDisabled & Non zero value indicates that antenna deployment is disabled. & 8 & 0 \\
    \bottomrule
\end{longtable}

\warn{This procedure is performed once after each OBC boot until it is disabled by the using \tcref{Set Antenna Deployment Mask} telecommand.}

Whenever there is a problem during performing any step it will be retried up to 3 times. This procedure will run no less than 3530s.

The total burn time (per controller): $5 \cdot 30s = 150s$\\
The total burn time (per resistor): $30s (auto) + 30s (override) = 60s$\\
\textit{Each resistor can tolerate a maximum of 100 burn cycles or a cumulated burn time of 300s.} - ICD\textsl{}
